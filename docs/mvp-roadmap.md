# MVP roadmap

## Validation v1 — this packet

- Config-driven campaign page and offer tiers.
- Stripe TEST one-time checkout intent for the quick and full tiers.
- Waitlist/checklist email capture with first-touch UTM attribution.
- First-party funnel events and pre-registered PASS/KILL query.
- Persist-first contact form with optional Resend notification.

## Explicitly out of scope

- Live payments, live ads, outbound email, domains, hosting, or external resource creation.
- The risk checklist asset itself; the page honestly promises delivery at launch.
- Risk-classification engine, Annex IV document generator, audit delivery workflow, monitoring deliverables, legal opinions, or specialist referral network.

## Gate before any live checkout

Implement and audit every promised deliverable, complete the operator's test-customer pass, define high-risk handoff and monitoring scope, clear legal review, then pass the Shell Corp product-delivers gate.
